Mountain Rafting Adventure

Our Mountain Rafting Adventure Tour is aimed at everyone who is looking for a sporting challenge. Rafting requires skill, coordination and fitness as well as team spirit and cooperation.

distance traveled: 7 kilometers

Duration: 3 - 3,5 hours (including dressing, transport, instructions)

Start: At 10 am and 2 pm

Minimum age: 14 years

Difficulty level: Intermediate

Preis pro Person: 300€

River Expedition

The River Expedition tour is particularly suitable for paddlers with experience, but also for brave newcomers with a certain sporting ambition. The Expedition Tour will only be operated when the water level is high.

distance traveled: 9 kilometers

Duration: 4 hours (including dressing, transport, instructions)

Start: At 10 am and 2 pm

Minimum age: 14 years

Difficulty level: Advanced

Preis pro Person: 500€

Family Fun Rafting

Our family tour is particularly suitable for adventurous families. The motto here is: one for all, all for one.

Minimum age: 10 years

Difficulty level: Easy
